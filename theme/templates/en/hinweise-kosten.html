Instructions for Login (LMU Members)

Login for LMU Members

Login is done through the LMU Single Sign-On.

Instructions for First-time Login

When logging in for the first time on the LMU Cloud Printing Portal, please check under My Profile to see if your card number is entered. If it is not entered, please add it under Change Card ID. Please make sure to enter it correctly; for the Mensa card, only enter the numeric sequence.

Login for Members of Other Universities

For first-time login, please click on the Create Account link and set up a new account by providing your email address and password. You will receive a confirmation email for activation.

Information on registration

  • Create an account once
  • In your account, go to My Profile under Change Card ID and provide the card number (pay attention to the correct format - enter only the numeric sequence, omit the letter at the beginning)
  • Save
