Information on registration

  • Register with the student union number on your LMUcard or cafeteria card.
  • Just enter the sequence of numbers,leave out the letter at the beginning.
  • Be careful not to make any typos, otherwise you will not be able to access your documents yourself someone else with that number could get access to it. You are responsible if sensitive data falls into the hands of others due to your incorrect entry advised.
