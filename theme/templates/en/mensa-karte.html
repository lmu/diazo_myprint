LMUcard / cafeteria card

To print and pay on the multi function printer / copier you need a chip card with a cash function from the Studierendenwerk München Oberbayern, these are the LMUcard or the Mensakarte (cafeteria card). Information about the LMUcard.

You can find the student union number on the back of your LMUcard / Mensakarte.

Canteen cards can be obtained from the relevant issuing points of the Studierendenwerk München Oberbayerns
