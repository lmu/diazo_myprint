LMUcard / cafeteria card

To print on the copier you need a chip card with a cash function from the Munich Student Union, these are the LMUcard and the cafeteria card. Information about the LMUcard.

You can find the student union number on the back of your LMUcard / cafeteria card.

Canteen cards can be obtained from the relevant issuing points of the Studentenwerk
